Spiritual Formation

What is Spiritual Formation?

We define Spiritual Formaiton as the process of being formed into the image of Christ for the sake of others and the glory of God

In Neo Impact we have adopted the language of Spiritual Formation. We believe that being formed is something that is inescapable. We are all shaped and formed by a mirad of things from our friend group, our family of origin to those we follow on social media. If we want to become like Jesus we must submit ourlves to a lifelong process of Spiritual Formation.
