Discipleship Framework

The word disciple in the original language is closer to our English word "apprentice". An apprentice is someone who closely studies under someone they are seeking to emulate. The 12 apostles in the New Testament followed Jesus around and sought to emulate his way of life. In Neo Impact we see our call in the 21st century to be the same. Discipleship for us to to apprentice under our teacher Jesus together. Some may be a little further along in their journey but we are all following the same Teacher. For us apprenticeship or discipleship can be thought of in three categories
1. Being with Jesus
2. Becoming like Jesus
3. Doing what Jesus did
