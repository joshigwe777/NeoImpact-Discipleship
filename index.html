Purpose

Here you will find content and recommended resources that will assist you in your discipleship journey. Mentors will find content to walk through in their discipleship groups and students being lead will find content for themsleves and that can be suggested for those whom they are lead by.

Just the beginning!

Spiritual Growth is a lifelong journey that for most of us is just beginning. Neo Impact is here to journey with you as we all seek to let the Holy Spirit shape and form our charater into his image. We call this lifelong process spiritual formation.

Why Ethnic Identity?

In Impact we believe that each person's ethnic identity is integral to who we are and who God is shaping us to be. Here you will find resources that will help assist you as you grow in your ethnic identity.
